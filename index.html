Hercules

About Hercules

Hercules is the son of Zeus and Hera who was kidnapped by Hades's minions, Pain and Panic as a baby. They then made him drink a potion which was suppose to transform him from a God to a human so that they could kill him. However, Hercules did not drink the entire potion as one drop was left in the bottle. Hercules was then transformed into a mortal with super strengh! He goes through life feeling like he never belongs anywhere until he embarks on a journey to become a real hero.

Hercules's Friends

Hercules has some friends who help him along the way. These friends include Pegasus, his winged horse that was gifted to him by his father when he was born, Philoctetes, his trainer and lastly, Megara, a woman he saves from a Centaur in his early career as a hero.
